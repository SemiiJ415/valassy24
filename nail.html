Nail Technician

We offer a hands-on instructional class, teaching the basics of a manicure, pedicure, Gel polish manicure, Nail Enhancements, Creative nail art, the latest nail trends in the industry, plus professional development.

Valassy Institute of Cosmetology students will have the unique opportunity to learn from licensed Estheticians, using cutting-edge skincare technology in a spa-like environment.

Program Description

The Valassy Institute of Cosmetology offers a program that has instructor-led classwooms PLUS clinical training as well as hands-on experience. Courses focus on theoretical and practical experience to help students learn professional skills that are vital to success. Each phase of the student's education includes a combination of learning approaches for students to become well-versed with hair and scalp treatments, hair care rinces, hair shaping, hair arranging, chemical waving and relaxing, hair coloring, facials, skin care, hair removal, manicuring/pedicuring, professional business-building skills in management and employability skills.

The last phase of our program offers students the opportunity to become advanced alongside licensed instructors within a real salon environment. Students will learn the latest styles and techniques in haircutting, hair stylist, and hair coloring.

Program Objectives

Our Nail Care program is designed to provide students with the necessary requirements needed to pass the Mississippi State Board of Cosmetology examination as well as prepare them for an entry level position in a salon or spa. The need for professional Nail Technicians to provide creative artistic, healthy and beauty solutions for the nails is growing significantly and our objective is to assure we train and educate the qualified candidates in the industry.
