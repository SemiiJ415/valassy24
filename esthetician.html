esthiology

Our Esthiology classes prepare you for a future as a skin care specialist. Graduates learn about the science and latest technological advancements for wellness and health. Training includes the proper method for facials, waxing, makeup application, professional development, wellness, and retail knowledge.

Program Description

The Valassy Institute of Cosmetology offers a program that has instructor-led classwooms PLUS clinical training as well as hands-on experience. Courses focus on theoretical and practical experience to help students learn professional skills that are vital to success. Each phase of the student's education includes a combination of learning about a complete spa experience. Students will read assigned texts, take quizzes, watch demonstrations, and practice skills in our state-of-the art clinic.

Program Objectives

The objectives of this Valassy Institute of Cosmetology's Esthiology program is designed to prepare students for the Mississippi State licensing examinations and provide the training that will prepare them to work in salons and spas that offer full service skin care treatments. Students will learn technical, personal, and professional skills - the Valassy way!

Paths for estheticians

  • Facial/Skin Care Specialist
  • Waxing Specialist
  • Spa Director
  • Wellness Consultant
  • Valassy Institute of Cosmetology Instructor
