When we say #BeValassy, it really embodies our belief in offering a world-class education and nurturing individuals to express their creativity and individuality while contributing to a multi-billion dollar beauty industry. We are building a community of Cosmetologists, Estheticians, and Nail Technicians that will influence global fashion and cultural trends. We also provide opportunities for entrepreneurship and personal branding, allowing professionals to redefine beauty standards and promote self-confidence in their community and throughout the world.
